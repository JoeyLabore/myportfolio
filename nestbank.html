Project Background

NestBank is a non-profit microfinance organisation dedicated to helping women in poverty build small businesses and achieve financial independence. Founded by Nobel Peace Prize laureate Muhammad Yunus, it provides affordable microloans, financial training, and support to women entrepreneurs across the United States, fostering economic inclusion and community growth.


  • Client: NestBank
  • Industry: Fintech
  • Duration: 2022–2023 (MVP)
  • My Role: UI & Brand Design Lead

Problem & Solution

Problem

NestBank’s loan management process relied heavily on in-person interactions and manual systems, limiting accessibility and scalability as membership grew across multiple states.


Solution

NestBank wanted to design a mobile-first platform that simplified loan applications, payments, and account management. The new app empowered members to take control of their finances while reducing operational inefficiencies.
